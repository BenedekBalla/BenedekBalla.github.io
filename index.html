About Me

Hi there! My name is Benedek Balla, I'm a fourth year undergraduate student at the University of Toronto studying computer science with a minor in statistics. I am fasctinated by machine learning research, and am always seeking opportunities to collaborate with and learn from creative, knowledgeable, and enthusiastic people so that together we can contribute original reserach to the field! Oh, and before I forget, thanks ChatGPT for helping with the HTML! :)

My mission is to enable more research and people to benefit from ML by making models scalable, robust, (hopefully) interpretable, and trustworthy problem-solvers across multiple data modalities.

Title : Generative AI for Medical Imaging

Timeline : September 2023 - May 2024

Supervisor : Pascal Tyrrell

Publication : Balla, B.; Hibi, A.; Tyrrell, P.N. Diffusion-Based Image Synthesis or Traditional Augmentation for Enriching Musculoskeletal Ultrasound Datasets. BioMedInformatics 2024, 4, 1934-1948.

Synthesized musculoskeletal ultrasound images with denoising diffusion probabilistic models and evaluated the synthetic images by training a convolutional neural network to diagnose thickened synovium.

To learn more, please see the paper I published here!

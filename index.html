Title : Generative AI for Medical Imaging

Timeline : September 2023 - May 2024

Supervisor : Pascal Tyrrell

Publication :

Balla, B.; Hibi, A.; Tyrrell, P.N. Diffusion-Based Image Synthesis or Traditional Augmentation for Enriching Musculoskeletal Ultrasound Datasets. BioMedInformatics 2024, 4, 1934-1948.

Over 8 months, I set out to improve the performance of a fine-tuned convolutional neural network at diagnosing thickened synovium and knee joint recess distension. To accomplish this, I decided to enrich its fine-tuning set using synthetically generated musculoskeletal ultrasound images from a denoising diffusion probabilistic model. To evaluate my methodology, I compared the CNN's performance when trained on a dataset enriched with synthetic images against enrichment with augmented images and no enrichment at all. My results suggest that synthetic dataset enrichment is prefereable in the domain of medical imaging, and the synthetic images encourage the model to learn representations aligned with human decision-making heuristics.

To learn more, please see the paper I published here!

What did I learn?

  1. Build deep learning models in Python with PyTorch and PyTorch Lightning.
  2. Run ML workloads on high performance computing clusters (Digital Research Alliance of Canada).
  3. Write a publishable research paper and read state-of-the-art ML research papers.
  4. Shell Script (An absolute life saver for managing datasets!).

Title : AI for Gate Detection in Autonomous Drone Racing

Timeline : May 2023 - September 2023

Supervisor : Chao Qin

Joined a team of undergraduate and graduate students to build a competitive autonomous racing drone. Using a shallow convolutional neural network, I was able to improve the latency of racing gate detection by over 10X while remaining robust to motion-blur and variable lighting conditions.

What did I learn?

  1. State-of-the-art learning-based computer vision algorithms (E.g. YOLO).
  2. The Robotics Operating System.
  3. Sim-to-real transfer learning for training neural networks.
